BRICS

Economic cooperation among emerging economies

Purpose

BRICS promotes economic cooperation among developing countries and supports a balanced global economy.

Member Countries

Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa, and newly added member nations.

Role in World Politics

BRICS strengthens the voice of developing nations and provides an alternative to Western-dominated institutions.
