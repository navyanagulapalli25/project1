United Nations (UN)

Maintaining international peace and security

Purpose

The United Nations was established in 1945 to promote international peace, security, and cooperation among countries.

Main Organs

General Assembly, Security Council, Economic and Social Council, International Court of Justice, and Secretariat.

Role in the World

The UN works on peacekeeping, human rights, climate change, sustainable development, and humanitarian aid.
