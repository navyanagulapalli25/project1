NATO

North Atlantic Treaty Organization

Purpose

NATO was established in 1949 to provide collective defense and ensure the security of its member countries.

Member Countries

NATO consists of 30+ member countries mainly from North America and Europe.

Role in Global Security

NATO promotes military cooperation, crisis management, and collective defense under Article 5.
