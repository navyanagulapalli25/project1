G20

Group of Twenty Major Economies

Purpose

The G20 is an international forum of major economies that works to promote global economic stability and sustainable development.

Member Countries

The G20 includes 19 countries and the European Union, representing the world's largest economies.

Role in Global Economy

The G20 discusses financial regulations, climate change, global trade, and economic growth strategies.
