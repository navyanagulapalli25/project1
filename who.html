World Health Organization (WHO)

Promoting global health and well-being

Purpose

WHO is a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health.

Main Focus Areas

Disease prevention, vaccination programs, emergency health response, mental health, and global health research.

Global Impact

WHO coordinates international health efforts and provides guidelines to improve healthcare systems worldwide.
